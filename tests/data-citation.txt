Data in test_dicom.zip comes from the C4KC-KiTS dataset of The Cancer Imaging
Archive (TCIA) and is licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported
License (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/)

The zip file contains a very small subset of the data in this study and is
intended to be used only for the purposes of testing dicom-numpy functionality.
